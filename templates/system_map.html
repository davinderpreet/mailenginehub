Shopify Sync
Nightly full sync pulls all customers, orders, and order items from Shopify API. Incremental sync runs more frequently for recent changes. Feeds fresh data into scoring.
app.py (scheduled jobs)
RFM Scoring Engine
Computes Recency-Frequency-Monetary scores for every contact. Determines customer value tiers. Uses purchase history, email engagement, and website activity as inputs.
ai_engine.py
—scored contacts
Customer Intelligence
Computes 50+ fields per customer: lifecycle stage, customer type, purchase intent, churn risk, optimal send window, LTV prediction, category affinity, reorder likelihood, and intelligence summary.
customer_intelligence.py
—profiles
—with intelligence
Next Best Message
Deterministic decision engine. Scores 10 action types per contact: reorder_reminder, cross_sell, education, upsell, winback, new_product, discount_offer, loyalty_reward, wait, switch_channel. Picks the highest-scoring non-cooldown action for each person.
next_best_message.py
—decisions
Campaign Planner
Aggregates individual decisions into campaign opportunities. Scores and ranks campaigns by expected impact. Runs preflight simulation to estimate deliverability and engagement.
campaign_planner.py
—plans generated

Auto-Scheduler + AI Personalization
Reads MessageDecision rows from the intelligence stage. When AI personalization is enabled, calls Claude Haiku to write a unique email per customer using their full profile, intelligence brief, category affinity, and purchase history. Falls back to template-based emails if disabled.
app.py (_run_auto_scheduler) ai_engine.py
—AI-generated
Flow Processor
Manages automated email flows: welcome, browse recovery, cart recovery, checkout recovery, post-purchase, winback, high-intent browse. Enrolls contacts, evaluates conditions at each step, and triggers email sends with proper delays and branching.
app.py (flow processor)
—active flows
—enrollments
Warmup & Delivery Engine
Controls sending volume with 8-phase warmup (50/day → unlimited). Checks suppression list, applies rate limits, and sends through Amazon SES. Delivery modes: live (SES), shadow (no SES), sandbox (5/day max).
delivery_engine.py email_sender.py
—warmup phase
—sent today
Template System
26 block types (hero, text, CTA, product grid, spec table, FAQ, etc.) compose into email templates. Block Registry validates and renders. Email shell wraps body content with branded header + CAN-SPAM footer.
block_registry.py email_templates.py email_shell.py
—templates
Discount Engine
Generates unique 6-character Shopify discount codes per customer via the Shopify Admin API. Codes are tied to email purpose (welcome, winback, etc.) and tracked for usage and revenue attribution.
discount_engine.py

Analyzes what worked: which action types, send times, and content styles drive engagement. Optimizes the strategy weights that feed back into Next Best Message scoring. Continuously improves decision quality based on real outcome data.
